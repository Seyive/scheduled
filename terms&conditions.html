Schedule conveyor is a unique company which accepts goods/equipments for carriage only upon that conditions listed below are met without any alteration.
Schedule conveyor offers a platform to connect users to conveyors.
It is obligatory for conveyor(truck owners) to provide transportation services which will be scheduled through the platform.
Schedule conveyor has no responsibility for any conveying service rendered to you outside the platform.

DEFINITION OF TERMS

  • USERS:

    The person or company who orders for the services of a conveyor.

  • CONVEYOR:

    The person or company to whom the user calls to deliver the goods.

  • CONSIGNMENT:

    This means the goods/equipments that would be transported from one point to another.


DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES

    Schedule conveyor provides a platform through web app, in which you can submit proposed conveyance.
    You must provide the following information as part of request for a convey:

    1. The origin and destination of the convey.
    2. Movement shall only commence when the conveyor takes possession of the goods.
    3. The specific equipment requirements and any other necessary information for safe and proper transportation of the equipment.

  • You shall also be responsible and liable for providing accurate description of equipment without limitation.
  • You should provide a proof of delivery signed by the client once convey is completed.
